# About

## About the Event

ACM CTF 2.0 is a cybersecurity competition hosted by the ACM Student Chapter at Prince Sultan University's College of Computer and Information Sciences. The event is designed to challenge students through practical security scenarios that combine offensive techniques, analytical thinking, and team collaboration.

By blending preparation workshops with a timed competition, ACM CTF 2.0 helps participants sharpen hands-on security skills and connect with a growing local cybersecurity community.
