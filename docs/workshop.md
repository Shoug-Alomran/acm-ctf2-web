## CTF Readiness Workshop: Core Offensive & Defensive Techniques

<span class="chip">ACM OFFICIAL SESSION</span>

A guided preparation session led by the ACM cybersecurity team. Participants will walk through practical workflows for approaching CTF tasks efficiently, documenting findings, and collaborating in teams under timed conditions.

### Learning Outcomes

- Build a repeatable methodology for solving CTF challenges.
- Use browser and terminal tools for web, crypto, and forensic triage.
- Identify high-value clues quickly and avoid common dead ends.
- Collaborate with role-based team workflow during timed sessions.
